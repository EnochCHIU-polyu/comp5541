## Summary
Alphabet Q1 2025 revenues increased 12% (14% constant currency) to $90.2B, with Google Services up 10% to $77.3B and Google Cloud up 28% to $12.3B. Operating income rose 20% with margin expanding 2pp to 34%. Net income grew 46% and diluted EPS improved 49% to $2.81. Free cash flow was $17.6B for the quarter.

## Overview
Strong performance across search, YouTube, subscriptions, and cloud driven by AI infrastructure and generative AI solutions. Operating margin expansion and robust cash flow highlight operational efficiency. Revenue growth decelerated slightly from 15% YoY in Q1 2024 to 12%, but constant currency growth remains solid at 14%.

## Further Direction
Cloud growth momentum and AI investments are key drivers. Continued focus on margin expansion and free cash flow generation. Risks include foreign exchange volatility, competition, and macroeconomic uncertainties as noted in forward-looking statements.
