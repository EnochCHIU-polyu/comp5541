## Summary
Alphabet's Q1 2025 earnings show solid revenue growth, improved operating margins, and strong free cash flow. Google Cloud profitability increased significantly. Forward-looking statements caution about risks, but no specific guidance provided.

## Overview
Revenue and operating income grew year-over-year, with operating margin expanding to 33.9% from 31.6%. Google Cloud operating margin jumped to 17.8% from 9.4%. Free cash flow remained robust, with TTM free cash flow of $39.1 billion. Capital expenditures increased. The company maintains a strong balance sheet.
