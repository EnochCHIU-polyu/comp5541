## Summary
Alphabet Q1 2026 revenues grew 22% to $109.9B, with operating income up 30% and net income up 81% to $62.6B (EPS $5.11). Google Cloud surged 63% to $20.0B. Dividend increased 5% to $0.22. Strong performance across Search, YouTube, Cloud, and AI initiatives.

## Overview
The quarter exceeded expectations with double-digit revenue growth across all major segments. Google Services rose 16% on Search strength; Google Cloud accelerated meaningfully on AI demand. Operating margin expanded 2pp to 36.1%. Net income benefited from $37.7B in unrealized gains on equity securities. The company issued $31.1B in debt and continues heavy AI investment.

## Further Direction
Alphabet is well-positioned with AI driving Search, Cloud, and subscription growth. However, the large non-recurring investment gains inflate net income; core operating performance remains robust. Continued capex and R&D spend will pressure margins but support long-term growth. Expect sustained momentum from AI monetization and Cloud backlog ($460B+).

## Summary
Alphabet reported strong Q2 2025 results with consolidated revenues of $96.4 billion, up 14% YoY (13% in constant currency). Google Cloud revenues surged 32% to $13.6 billion, while Search, YouTube ads, and subscriptions all delivered double-digit growth. Net income rose 19% to $28.2 billion, and diluted EPS increased 22% to $2.31. Operating margin was 32.4%, and the company raised its 2025 capital expenditure guidance to approximately $85 billion to meet growing demand for AI and cloud products.

## Overview
The quarter demonstrates robust momentum across Alphabet's core businesses, with AI integration driving performance in Search, YouTube, and Cloud. Cloud annual revenue run-rate now exceeds $50 billion. Operating income grew 14%, and net income increased 19%. The company issued $12.5 billion in senior unsecured notes in May 2025 for general corporate purposes. Total cash, cash equivalents, and marketable securities stood at $95.1 billion. Employee count rose to 187,103 from 179,582 a year ago.

## Further Direction
Alphabet plans to continue aggressive investment in AI infrastructure and cloud capabilities, with increased capex guidance of $85 billion for 2025. The company is focused on expanding AI Overviews, AI Mode, and generative AI solutions. Legal settlements and ongoing regulatory risks remain, but the strong revenue growth and expanding cloud profitability suggest sustained focus on high-growth areas. Further expansion in YouTube subscriptions and enterprise cloud services is expected.
