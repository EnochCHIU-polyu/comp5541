## Summary
Alphabet Q1 2026 revenues grew 22% to $109.9B, with operating income up 30% and net income up 81% to $62.6B (EPS $5.11). Google Cloud surged 63% to $20.0B. Dividend increased 5% to $0.22. Strong performance across Search, YouTube, Cloud, and AI initiatives.

## Overview
The quarter exceeded expectations with double-digit revenue growth across all major segments. Google Services rose 16% on Search strength; Google Cloud accelerated meaningfully on AI demand. Operating margin expanded 2pp to 36.1%. Net income benefited from $37.7B in unrealized gains on equity securities. The company issued $31.1B in debt and continues heavy AI investment.

## Further Direction
Alphabet is well-positioned with AI driving Search, Cloud, and subscription growth. However, the large non-recurring investment gains inflate net income; core operating performance remains robust. Continued capex and R&D spend will pressure margins but support long-term growth. Expect sustained momentum from AI monetization and Cloud backlog ($460B+).
