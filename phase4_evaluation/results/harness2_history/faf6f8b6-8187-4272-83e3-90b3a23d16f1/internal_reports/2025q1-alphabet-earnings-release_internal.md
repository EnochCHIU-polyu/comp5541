## Summary
Alphabet delivered a strong Q1 2025 with 12% revenue growth, 46% net income growth, and operating margin expansion to 34%. Google Cloud surged 28% and AI initiatives like Gemini 2.5 drove engagement. The company also increased dividend by 5% and authorized a $70B buyback.

## Overview
Consolidated revenues of $90.2B, beating expectations, with Google Services up 10% and Cloud up 28%. Operating income grew 20% to $30.6B. Net income of $34.5B included an $8B unrealized gain on equity securities. AI Overviews reached 1.5B monthly users, and YouTube/Google One subscriptions exceeded 270M. Capital returns enhanced via dividend hike and large buyback authorization.

## Further Direction
Continued focus on AI innovation across Search, Cloud, and subscriptions. Strong cash flow generation supports aggressive capital returns. Key risks include regulatory challenges, competition in AI and cloud, and potential macro slowdown. However, momentum from AI monetization and cloud growth suggests sustained outperformance.
