# Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is a type of deep neural network designed for processing structured grid data, such as images. It typically consists of convolutional layers, pooling layers, activation functions, and fully-connected layers. CNNs are effective for tasks like image recognition due to their ability to learn spatial hierarchies of features.
