# Pooling Layer

A pooling layer (e.g., MaxPooling) downsamples feature maps by summarizing the presence of features in patches, reducing spatial dimensions and computational load. This helps achieve translation invariance and control overfitting by providing a form of abstraction. Pooling is typically applied after convolutional and activation layers in a CNN architecture.
