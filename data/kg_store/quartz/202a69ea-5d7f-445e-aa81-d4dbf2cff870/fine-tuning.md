# Fine-Tuning

Fine-tuning is a transfer learning step where pre-trained model layers are unfrozen and trained with a new dataset, typically using a lower learning rate (e.g., 1/10 of the original). The extent of fine-tuning depends on dataset size; larger datasets allow more layers to be trained.
